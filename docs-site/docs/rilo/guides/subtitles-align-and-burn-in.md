title: "Subtitles: Align and Burn-in"
---

Rilo offers optional subtitle post-processing:
- alignSubtitles: runs ffsubsync to better match narration timing
- burnInSubtitles: applies ASS subtitles into final media

Enable these when always-visible captions are required by downstream platforms.
